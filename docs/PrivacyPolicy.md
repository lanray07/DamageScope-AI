# DamageScope AI Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 26, 2026

DamageScope AI is designed to help contractors, landlords, property managers, roofers, maintenance teams, fleet managers, insurance support teams, and restoration businesses document visible damage evidence and generate repair documentation.

## Information You Add

The app may store information you enter, including damage case titles, client names, property or vehicle locations, notes, photo labels, captions, repair priority notes, AI finding approvals, and locally generated reports.

The app may also access photos you choose from your photo library and images you capture with the camera. Photo access is used only to attach evidence to damage cases and generate documentation inside the app.

## Local Storage

DamageScope AI is offline-friendly. Case data, photo evidence, AI findings, and generated report records are stored locally on your device using Apple platform storage technologies unless you choose to share or export them.

## AI Processing

Mock AI mode is enabled by default. In mock mode, photos and case notes are processed locally using sample responses and are not sent to an external AI service.

The app includes a placeholder for a secure backend AI endpoint. If a future production backend is enabled, uploaded photos, location labels, damage type, and user notes may be sent to that backend solely to produce damage documentation suggestions. API keys must never be stored in the iOS app.

## Subscriptions

Auto-renewable subscriptions are processed by Apple through StoreKit. DamageScope AI does not receive or store your full payment card details.

## Sharing and Exports

You can generate PDF reports and share them using the native iOS share sheet. You control where exported reports are sent.

## Safety Disclaimer

AI findings are visual suggestions only. They are not insurance advice, legal advice, structural certification, or a repair cost guarantee. Findings should be reviewed by qualified professionals. Urgent safety concerns should be checked by professionals immediately.
