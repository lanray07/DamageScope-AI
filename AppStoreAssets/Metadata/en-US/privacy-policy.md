# DamageScope AI Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 22, 2026

DamageScope AI is designed for local damage documentation, photo evidence organisation, AI-assisted visual suggestions, and PDF report generation.

## Data Stored On Device

Damage cases, notes, photo evidence, AI finding approvals, report records, and generated PDF paths are stored locally on the user's device using Apple platform storage.

## Photos

The app can access photos selected by the user and can use the camera when the user chooses to capture evidence. Selected or captured images are used for damage case documentation and report generation.

## AI Processing

Mock AI mode is enabled by default in the current app build. In mock mode, photos and case details are not transmitted to DamageScope AI servers.

If a future production backend is configured, uploaded damage photos and related case notes may be sent to a secure backend endpoint solely to generate visual damage documentation suggestions. API keys must not be stored inside the iOS app.

## Subscriptions

Subscription purchases are processed by Apple through StoreKit. DamageScope AI does not receive or store payment card details.

## No Professional Advice

AI findings are visual suggestions only. DamageScope AI does not provide insurance advice, legal advice, structural certification, safety clearance, or repair cost guarantees.

## Contact

For support, use the support page linked in the App Store listing.
